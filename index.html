THE BRAINROT COLLECTION

Italian brainrot is an internet meme that emerged in early 2025. It’s characterized by absurd and ironic photos and videos featuring AI-generated creatures with pseudo-Italian names, such as Tralalero Tralala.

This phenomenon quickly spread on TikTok and Instagram due to a combination of synthesized “Italian” voiceovers, grotesque visuals, and nonsensical narratives. The term “brain rot” is used to describe content that is both repulsive and addictive.

The animals and creatures associated with the Italian brain rot phenomenon are images generated by neural networks and visualized in an absurd, deliberately ridiculous manner 1. Typically, these are hybrids of animals, household objects, food, weapons, and fantastical elements, combined under deliberately Italianized names.
